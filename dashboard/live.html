K sampled trades per step (default 200 of the ~200K processed). Newest first. Filter by pair type or executed/rejected. "ƒ" is friction; "+" is real surplus delivered; "✕" is the gate that killed the trade.

WebGL particle field via deck.gl. Each sampled pair launches a particle that travels from its origin sector to its destination over ~900ms, leaving an arc trail. Particle colour encodes pair type (H↔H green, H↔A accent, A↔A blue); trail colour follows the origin sector palette. Width scales with the prototype-pair's real-world weight.

Twelve sectors arranged around a circle. Ribbon thickness encodes the weighted volume of cleared trades between sector pairs, derived from the K-pair-per-step sample. Per-step mode shows the instantaneous snapshot; cumulative mode shows the structure that emerges over the whole run.

Each column is one step. Within a column, bars stack from the base (depth 1, accent) to the top (deepest, red). Column height is logarithmic in step total fold-nominal; a Hawkes overshoot lights up its deepest bar. Folding-off scenarios show empty columns.
